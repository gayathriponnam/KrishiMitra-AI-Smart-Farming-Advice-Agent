=== RICE (Oryza sativa) ===
Seasons: Kharif (June-July transplanting), Rabi (some southern states)
Soil: Well-drained loamy to clay loam, pH 5.5-7.0
Water: 1200-1400mm (standing water in paddy fields)
Varieties:
  - High-Yielding: IR36, IR64, Swarna (MTU 7029), BPT 5204 (Samba Mahsuri)
  - Drought tolerant: Sahbhagi Dhan, DRR Dhan 42
  - Flood tolerant: Swarna-Sub1, CR Dhan 801
Fertilizer: N:P:K = 120:60:60 kg/ha
  - Apply 1/3 N + full P + full K at basal
  - 1/3 N at active tillering (25-30 days)
  - 1/3 N at panicle initiation
Pest Management:
  - Brown Plant Hopper (BPH): Imidacloprid 17.8 SL @ 100ml/acre or BPMC
  - Blast: Tricyclazole 75 WP @ 300g/ha
  - Sheath Blight: Hexaconazole 5 EC @ 2ml/L water
Harvest: 25-30% moisture content at harvest; thresh within 24 hours

=== WHEAT (Triticum aestivum) ===
Season: Rabi (October-November sowing, March-April harvest)
Soil: Loamy to clay loam, pH 6.0-7.5
Water: 450-650mm (4-6 irrigations)
Varieties:
  - HD 2967, HD 3086, PBW 343, GW 322, K 0307
  - Heat-tolerant: HD 2781, NW 1014
Fertilizer: N:P:K = 120:60:40 kg/ha
  - Basal: Full P, full K, 40kg N
  - CRI stage (3 weeks): 40kg N
  - Jointing stage: 40kg N
Irrigation Schedule:
  1. Crown Root Initiation (CRI): 20-25 DAS
  2. Tillering: 40-45 DAS
  3. Jointing: 65-70 DAS
  4. Flowering: 85-90 DAS
  5. Milky grain: 100-105 DAS
  6. Dough stage: 115-120 DAS
Pest: Yellow rust (Puccinia striiformis) — Propiconazole 25 EC @ 500ml/ha

=== COTTON (Gossypium hirsutum) ===
Season: Kharif (April-June sowing, harvested Oct-Jan)
Soil: Deep black cotton soil (Vertisols), pH 6.0-8.0
Varieties: Bt cotton hybrids — Bollgard-II (Cry1Ac + Cry2Ab)
  - Brahma, Bunny BG-II, MRC 7017 BG II, NCS 145 BG II
Spacing: 90 x 60 cm or 90 x 45 cm
Fertilizer: N:P:K = 100:50:50 kg/ha
Pest Management:
  - Bollworm (Helicoverpa, Spodoptera): Now resistant to Bt — Spinosad, Emamectin
  - Whitefly (Bemisia tabaci): Pyriproxyfen + Bifenazate; reduce Neonicotinoids
  - Pink Bollworm: Pheromone traps (1 trap/acre); Cyhalothrin if threshold crossed
IPM Strategy: Use pheromone traps, yellow sticky cards, neem-based sprays (NSKE 5%)

=== SOYBEAN (Glycine max) ===
Season: Kharif (June-July sowing)
Major States: Madhya Pradesh (53%), Maharashtra (30%), Rajasthan
Soil: Well-drained medium black soil, pH 6.0-7.0
Varieties: JS 335, JS 9305, NRC 37, MACS 450
Fertilizer: N:P:K = 20:60:40 kg/ha (low N as legume fixes nitrogen)
Rhizobium inoculant: Apply Bradyrhizobium japonicum @ 5g/kg seed
Pest: Girdle Beetle, Stem Fly, Pod Borer — use Chlorantraniliprole
Disease: Yellow Mosaic Virus (YMV) — white fly vector; use YMV-resistant varieties

=== SUGARCANE (Saccharum officinarum) ===
Season: Two seasons: Oct-Nov (Autumn) and Feb-March (Spring planting)
Crop Duration: 10-12 months (plant crop), 8-10 months (ratoon)
Soil: Deep, well-drained loam, pH 6.5-7.5
Varieties: Co 86032, CoJ 64, Co 0238, VCF 0517
Fertilizer: N:P:K = 250:100:120 kg/ha
Water: 1500-2500mm; highly water-intensive
Harvest: BRIX reading 18-20%; juice recovery 10-11%
Ratoon: Apply 25% extra N to ratoon crop

=== MAIZE (Zea mays) ===
Seasons: Kharif (June), Rabi (Oct-Nov in south India), Spring (Feb in hills)
Varieties: Pioneer 30V92, DKC 9144, HQPM 1 (QPM for nutrition)
Soil: Well-drained loamy, pH 6.0-7.5
Fertilizer: N:P:K = 150:60:60 kg/ha
  - Basal: 1/3 N + full P + full K
  - Knee-height: 1/3 N
  - Tasseling: 1/3 N
Pest: Fall Armyworm (Spodoptera frugiperda) — apply Emamectin benzoate 5 SG @ 220g/ha
      into whorl when leaf damage >10%

=== CHICKPEA / GRAM (Cicer arietinum) ===
Season: Rabi (October-November sowing)
Soil: Well-drained sandy loam, pH 6.0-8.0 (tolerates slightly alkaline)
Varieties: JG 11, ICCC 37, KAK 2, Pusa 256
Fertilizer: N:P:K = 20:60:20 kg/ha (seed treatment with Rhizobium essential)
Irrigation: 1-2 supplemental irrigations (pre-flowering and pod-filling)
Disease: Fusarium Wilt — use resistant varieties; seed treat with Carbendazim 50 WP
Pod Borer (Helicoverpa): Bt formulation (Bt kurstaki) or Emamectin benzoate

=== MUSTARD (Brassica juncea) ===
Season: Rabi (Sep-Oct sowing in north India)
Varieties: Pusa Bold, Pusa Mustard 21, Pusa Mustard 25, Pusa Agrani
Fertilizer: N:P:K = 60:30:0 kg/ha + Sulphur 30 kg/ha
Irrigation: 2-3 irrigations (germination, flowering, pod filling)
Aphid Management: Spray Dimethoate 30 EC @ 1L/ha if >25 aphids/plant

=== POTATO (Solanum tuberosum) ===
Season: Rabi (Oct-Nov planting, Jan-Feb harvest)
Soil: Well-drained sandy loam with high organic matter, pH 5.2-6.4
Seed Rate: 20-25 quintals/ha (45-55g seed tubers)
Varieties: Kufri Pukhraj, Kufri Jyoti, Kufri Chandramukhi, Kufri Badshah
Fertilizer: N:P:K = 180:100:100 kg/ha
  Split N: 1/3 at planting, 1/3 at earthing up, 1/3 45 days after planting
Disease: Late Blight (Phytophthora infestans) — Mancozeb 75 WP @ 2kg/ha preventive;
         Metalaxyl + Mancozeb curative
Harvest: When 75% of foliage yellows; allow 10 days skin-set before harvest

=== ONION (Allium cepa) ===
Seasons: Kharif (June-July nursery), Late Kharif (Aug-Sep), Rabi (Dec-Jan)
Varieties: Bhima Raj, Bhima Super, Agrifound Dark Red, N 53
Fertilizer: N:P:K = 100:50:50 kg/ha
Pest: Thrips (Thrips tabaci) — Spinosad 45 SC @ 3ml/10L; fipronil
Disease: Purple Blotch — Mancozeb; Downy Mildew — Metalaxyl
Storage: Cool, dry, well-ventilated conditions; 65-70% RH reduces rotting
